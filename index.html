Finance · Central Banking · FinTech · Research Infrastructure

Research on financial technology, financial markets, and open analytical tools.

I am Yue Dai, a PhD in FinTech from Renmin University of China and an economist and postdoctoral researcher at the Research Institute of the People's Bank of China.

My research agenda is organized around three interconnected areas:

(1) macroeconomic research, policy analysis, and the automation of economic research with AI;

(2) algorithmic trading strategy design across multiple asset classes, including equities, bonds, funds, commodities, and derivatives, with an emphasis on low-frequency investment research;

(3) high-frequency trading strategies, market microstructure, and the broader market implications of technology-driven trading.

This page presents selected public research outputs and non-sensitive research tools, including reproducible workflows, policy-research templates, and technical notes intended to support open collaboration.

Open Repositories

These repositories are intended to document reproducible research workflows while excluding private data, unpublished full texts, and sensitive implementation details.

Central Banking

central-bank-fintech-briefings

Public-source briefings on how central banks and financial authorities adopt new technologies, including AI, data infrastructure, digital currency, suptech, regtech, and alternative data.

  • Briefings based on public central bank reports, speeches, and working papers
  • Comparative notes on AI, CBDC, data governance, and supervision technology
  • Reusable templates for policy research, technology scans, and risk matrices
Repository
Economic Nowcasting

economic-nowcasting

A satellite-image-based economic nowcasting workflow using remote sensing and alternative data, with a focus on monthly macro indicators and selected firm-level signals.

  • Monthly macroeconomic nowcasting using satellite imagery, nighttime lights, land surface temperature, and atmospheric signals
  • Firm-level extensions for revenue, profit, and equity-market related indicators
  • Remote-sensing pixel, enterprise, industry, and macro aggregation pipeline
Repository
Financial LLMs

finllm-research-toolkit

A research toolkit for finance-oriented large language models, including paper taxonomy, task design, evaluation rubrics, RAG examples, agent workflow notes, and small public demos.

  • Financial LLM literature review and taxonomy
  • Evaluation templates for financial text understanding
  • Reproducible RAG and agent workflow examples
Repository
HFT and Market Technology

hft-strategy-market-impact

A technical and empirical project on high-frequency trading strategies, high-frequency data infrastructure, and the market-wide impact of algorithmic trading.

  • Tick, order-book, intraday, and overnight return data pipelines
  • Strategy research: momentum/reversal signals, liquidity cycles, and execution effects
  • Technical notes on C++, low-latency processing, concurrency, and large-scale market data
